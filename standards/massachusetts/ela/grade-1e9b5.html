RF.1 Foundational Skills

  • Print Concepts

    • RF.1.1 Demonstrate understanding of the organization and basic features of print.

      • Choose the sentence that is spaced correctly (K-C.2)
      • Find a word in a sentence (K-C.3)
      • Identify book parts and features (K-R.1)

      • RF.1.1.a Recognize the distinguishing features of a sentence (e.g., first word, capitalization, ending punctuation).

        • Find the complete sentences (1-EE.9)
        • Unscramble the words to make a complete sentence (1-EE.11)
        • Capitalize sentences and the pronoun "I" (1-PP.3)

  • Phonological Awareness

    • RF.1.2 Demonstrate understanding of spoken words, syllables, and sounds (phonemes).

      • How many syllables does the word have? (1-B.1)
      • Sort by the number of syllables (1-B.2)
      • Choose the picture that rhymes with the word (1-C.1)
      • Which two words start with the same sound? (1-E.1)
      • Which two words end with the same sound? (1-E.2)
      • Put two syllables together to create a word (1-R.1)

      • RF.1.2.a Distinguish long from short vowel sounds in spoken single-syllable words.

        • Sort short and long vowel words (1-M.1)
        • Match the short a and long a words to pictures (1-M.2)
        • Match the short e and long e words to pictures (1-M.3)
        • Choose the short i or long i word that matches the picture (1-M.4)
        • Choose the short o or long o word that matches the picture (1-M.5)
        • Choose the short u or long u word that matches the picture (1-M.6)
        • Use spelling patterns to sort long and short vowel words (1-M.7)

      • RF.1.2.b Orally produce single-syllable words by blending sounds (phonemes), including consonant blends.

        • Blend the sounds together to make a word (1-D.1)

      • RF.1.2.c Isolate and pronounce initial, medial vowel, and final sounds (phonemes) in spoken single-syllable words.

        • Identify each sound in a word (1-D.2)
        • Put the sounds in order (1-D.3)
        • Which letter does the word start with? (1-E.3)
        • Which letter does the word end with? (1-E.4)
        • Complete the word with the right initial consonant blend (1-F.1)
        • Complete the word with the right final consonant blend (1-F.3)
        • Choose the correct digraph (1-F.7)
        • Complete the word with the right digraph (1-F.8)
        • Sort by initial consonant blend or digraph (1-F.10)
        • Complete the short a word (1-G.2)
        • Complete the short e word (1-H.2)
        • Complete the short i words (1-I.2)
        • Complete the short o word (1-J.2)
        • Complete the short u word (1-K.2)
        • Identify the short vowel sound in a word (1-L.1)
        • Complete the word with the right short vowel (1-L.2)
        • Complete the silent e words (1-N.2)
        • Complete the vowel team words (1-O.2)
        • Complete the word with the right r-controlled vowel: ar, er, ir, or, ur (1-P.2)
        • Complete the word with the right diphthong: oi, oy, ou, ow (1-Q.2)

      • RF.1.2.d Segment spoken single-syllable words into their complete sequence of individual sounds (phonemes).

        • Identify each sound in a word (1-D.2)
        • Put the sounds in order (1-D.3)

  • Phonics and Word Recognition

    • RF.1.3 Know and apply grade-level phonics and word analysis skills in decoding words.

      • Complete the word with the right initial consonant blend (1-F.1)
      • Complete the word with the right final consonant blend (1-F.3)
      • Fill in the missing consonant blend (1-F.5)
      • Complete the short a word (1-G.2)
      • Choose the short a sentence that matches the picture (1-G.3)
      • Complete the short e word (1-H.2)
      • Choose the short e sentence that matches the picture (1-H.3)
      • Complete the short i words (1-I.2)
      • Choose the short i sentence that matches the picture (1-I.3)
      • Complete the short o word (1-J.2)
      • Choose the short o sentence that matches the picture (1-J.3)
      • Complete the short u word (1-K.2)
      • Choose the short u sentence that matches the picture (1-K.3)
      • Identify the short vowel sound in a word (1-L.1)
      • Complete the word with the right short vowel (1-L.2)
      • Complete the sentence with the correct short vowel word (1-L.4)
      • Complete the word with the right r-controlled vowel: ar, er, ir, or, ur (1-P.2)
      • Complete the word with the right diphthong: oi, oy, ou, ow (1-Q.2)

      • RF.1.3.a Know the spelling-sound correspondences for common consonant digraphs.

        • Complete the word to match the picture: -ss, -ll, -ff, -zz, -ck (1-F.6)
        • Choose the correct digraph (1-F.7)
        • Complete the word with the right digraph (1-F.8)
        • Spell the digraph word (1-F.9)
        • Sort by initial consonant blend or digraph (1-F.10)

      • RF.1.3.b Decode regularly spelled one-syllable words.

        • Does the word start with a consonant blend? (1-F.2)
        • Does the word end with a consonant blend? (1-F.4)
        • Choose the short a word that matches the picture (1-G.1)
        • Choose the picture that matches the short e word (1-H.1)
        • Choose the short i word that matches the picture (1-I.1)
        • Choose the short o word that matches the picture (1-J.1)
        • Choose the picture that matches the short u word (1-K.1)
        • Match the short a and long a words to pictures (1-M.2)
        • Match the short e and long e words to pictures (1-M.3)
        • Choose the short i or long i word that matches the picture (1-M.4)
        • Choose the short o or long o word that matches the picture (1-M.5)
        • Choose the short u or long u word that matches the picture (1-M.6)
        • Choose the silent e word that matches the picture (1-N.1)
        • Choose the picture that matches the vowel team word (1-O.1)
        • Choose the r-control word that matches the picture (1-P.1)
        • Choose the diphthong word that matches the picture (1-Q.1)

      • RF.1.3.c Know final -e and common vowel team conventions for representing long vowel sounds.

        • Match the short a and long a words to pictures (1-M.2)
        • Match the short e and long e words to pictures (1-M.3)
        • Choose the short i or long i word that matches the picture (1-M.4)
        • Choose the short o or long o word that matches the picture (1-M.5)
        • Choose the short u or long u word that matches the picture (1-M.6)
        • Use spelling patterns to sort long and short vowel words (1-M.7)
        • Choose the silent e word that matches the picture (1-N.1)
        • Complete the silent e words (1-N.2)
        • Spell the silent e word (1-N.3)
        • Choose the silent e sentence that matches the picture (1-N.4)
        • Choose the picture that matches the vowel team word (1-O.1)
        • Complete the vowel team words (1-O.2)
        • Complete the word with the right vowel team (1-O.3)
        • Choose the vowel team sentence that matches the picture (1-O.4)

      • RF.1.3.d Use knowledge that every syllable must have a vowel sound to determine the number of syllables in a printed word.

        • Find the vowels in a word (1-A.2)
        • How many syllables does the word have? (1-B.1)
        • Put two syllables together to create a word (1-R.1)

      • RF.1.3.e Decode two-syllable words following basic patterns by breaking the words into syllables.

        • Complete the two-syllable words (1-R.2)
        • Complete the sentence with a two-syllable word (1-R.3)

      • RF.1.3.f Read words with inflectional endings.

        • Select the sentence that tells about the past (1-JJ.2)
        • Complete the verb with the ending that you hear (1-JJ.8)
        • Match the -ed and -ing sentences to the pictures (1-JJ.9)

      • RF.1.3.g Recognize and read grade-appropriate irregularly spelled words.

        • Choose the sight word that you hear (1-S.1)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct sight word (1-S.2)

  • Fluency

    • RF.1.4 Read with sufficient accuracy and fluency to support comprehension.

      • RF.1.4.a Read on-level text with purpose and understanding.

        • Complete the rhyme (1-C.3)
        • Complete the poem with a word that rhymes (1-C.4)
        • Choose the short a sentence that matches the picture (1-G.3)
        • Choose the short e sentence that matches the picture (1-H.3)
        • Choose the short i sentence that matches the picture (1-I.3)
        • Choose the short o sentence that matches the picture (1-J.3)
        • Choose the short u sentence that matches the picture (1-K.3)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct short vowel word (1-L.4)
        • Choose the silent e sentence that matches the picture (1-N.4)
        • Choose the vowel team sentence that matches the picture (1-O.4)
        • What am I? (1-V.3)

      • RF.1.4.b Read on-level text orally with accuracy, appropriate rate, and expression on successive readings.

      • RF.1.4.c Use context to confirm or self-correct word recognition and understanding, rereading as necessary.

        • Choose the silent e sentence that matches the picture (1-N.4)
        • Choose the vowel team sentence that matches the picture (1-O.4)
        • Complete the sentence with a two-syllable word (1-R.3)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct sight word (1-S.2)
        • Use context to identify the meaning of a word (1-CC.1)
        • Use action verbs (1-HH.3)
        • Use sense words (1-LL.1)
