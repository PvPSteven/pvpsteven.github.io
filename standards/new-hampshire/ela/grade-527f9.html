5.RF.5 Foundational Skills

  • 5 Print Concepts

  • 5 Phonological Awareness

  • 5 Phonics and Word Recognition

    • 5.RF.5.3 Know and apply grade-level phonics and word analysis skills in decoding words.

      • 5.RF.5.3a Use combined knowledge of all letter-sound correspondences, syllabication patterns, and morphology (e.g., roots and affixes) to read accurately unfamiliar multisyllabic words in context and out of context.

        • Words with pre- (5-P.1)
        • Words with re- (5-P.2)
        • Words with sub- (5-P.3)
        • Words with mis- (5-P.4)
        • Words with un-, dis-, in-, im-, and non- (5-P.5)
        • Words with -ful (5-P.6)
        • Words with -less (5-P.7)
        • Words with -able and -ible (5-P.8)
        • Sort words with shared prefixes and suffixes by meaning (5-P.9)
        • Sort words with shared suffixes by part of speech (5-P.10)
        • Word pattern analogies (5-P.11)
        • Word pattern sentences (5-P.12)
        • Sort words by shared Greek or Latin roots (5-Q.1)
        • Use Greek and Latin roots as clues to the meanings of words (5-Q.2)
        • Use the meanings of words as clues to the meanings of Greek and Latin roots (5-Q.3)
        • Use words as clues to the meanings of Greek and Latin roots (5-Q.4)
        • Determine the meanings of Greek and Latin roots (5-Q.5)
        • Determine the meanings of words with Greek and Latin roots (5-Q.6)
        • Match words with Greek and Latin roots to their meanings (5-Q.7)

  • 5 Fluency

    • 5.RF.5.4 Read with sufficient accuracy and fluency to support comprehension.

      • 5.RF.5.4a Read on-level text with purpose and understanding.

        • Determine the main idea (5-A.1)
        • Determine the order of events in informational texts (5-C.1)
        • Compare and contrast in informational texts (5-C.2)
        • Match causes and effects in informational texts (5-C.3)
        • Match problems with their solutions (5-C.4)
        • Identify sensory details (5-D.2)
        • Compare and contrast characters (5-F.2)
        • Determine the themes of short stories (5-G.1)
        • Identify story elements (5-G.2)
        • Analyze short stories (5-G.4)
        • Read and understand informational passages (5-H.1)
        • Compare information from two texts (5-H.3)
        • Identify supporting details in literary texts (5-L.4)
        • Identify supporting details in informational texts (5-L.5)

      • 5.RF.5.4b Read on-level prose and poetry orally with accuracy, appropriate rate, and expression on successive readings.

      • 5.RF.5.4c Use context to confirm or self-correct word recognition and understanding, rereading as necessary.

        • Similes and metaphors with pictures (5-E.3)
        • Determine the meanings of similes and metaphors (5-E.4)
        • Interpret the meaning of an allusion from its source (5-E.5)
        • Vocabulary review: Identify story elements (5-G.3)
        • Vocabulary review: Analyze short stories (5-G.5)
        • Vocabulary review: Read and understand informational passages (5-H.2)
        • Put the sentences in order (5-K.1)
        • Which definition matches the sentence? (5-V.2)
        • Which sentence matches the definition? (5-V.3)
        • Determine the meaning of idioms from context: set 1 (5-W.1)
        • Determine the meaning of idioms from context: set 2 (5-W.3)
        • Find words using context (5-Y.1)
        • Determine the meaning of words using synonyms in context (5-Y.2)
        • Use context to identify the meaning of a word (5-Y.3)
        • Determine the meaning of domain-specific words with pictures (5-Y.4)
