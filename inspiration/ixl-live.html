IXL, paired with professional learning, has led to proven success

IXL is already used by schools in 95 of the top 100 districts

IXL Gives Teachers and Students More Time
Teachers have to squeeze a lot of learning into one school year, and IXL gives them extra time. Independent research conducted in Oregon found that use of IXL produced academic gains on a statewide assessment equivalent to 6.2 weeks of additional instruction time over the course of a school year.
Empirical Education. (2013). A Study of Student Achievement, Teacher Perceptions, and IXL Math
Positive Outcomes on Standardized Tests
In an independent study of a Title I school in California, a group of low-performing 4th graders saw significant gains on state assessments when placed in a before-school math lab where IXL Math was the only intervention product used. During the two-year study, researchers found that students who worked in the lab each school day showed an overall 23-point increase on the California Standardized Test.
Donawerth, A. N. (2013). Bridging the Gap: Fourth Grade Before-School Computer Math Lab and Its Impact on California Standardized Test Scores
A High School Turnaround
After being ranked in the bottom 5 percent of public schools in the state, a Kentucky school implemented a comprehensive turnaround plan. Educators used IXL Math as the only math product for its redesigned RTI program, resulting in a dramatic improvement: after just two years, the school climbed to the 97th percentile and is now designated a "School of Distinction" by the state.
Stobaugh, Chandler, & White (2015). High School Turnaround: A Case Study
More Practice Equals Higher Test Scores
The more students practice on IXL, the higher they score on standardized tests. A study of thousands of California public schools indicated that if every student at a school mastered just 1 additional IXL skill per week, the school would see a nearly 10-point increase in its percent proficiency on the California Assessment of Student Performance and Progress (CAASPP).
IXL efficacy research. (2016). The IXL Effect: Measuring the Impact of IXL Math and IXL Language Arts in California Schools

FAQ

Who is eligible to attend an IXL Live event?
We welcome all teachers who would like to learn more about IXL! We'll take a deep dive into the most important parts of IXL (including newer features such as the Continuous Diagnostic) and explore practical, powerful ways they can solve challenges in your unique classroom. Whether you're just starting to explore or you've been using IXL for years, IXL Live will help you take it to the next level!
Do I need to have an IXL account or license to participate?
No, you do not need to have an IXL account for the event.
When do I need to register by?
Registration will be open until 3 days prior to the event, as long as spots remain available. We offer a limited number of tickets to ensure personalized attention, so IXL Live workshops do fill up quickly! Please register as soon as possible to guarantee your spot.
The event I want to attend is sold out—what can I do?
You can add yourself to the waitlist via the Eventbrite page for the event by clicking "Register" and then "Join Waitlist." You'll be notified if spots open up! We may also add new events near you, so please sign up to be notified about future workshops.
I don't see any events in my city! Am I out of luck?
Please sign up to be notified about future events in your area as we may add new events across the country.
Can I pay with a purchase order?
Yes, we do accept purchase orders! Please e-mail your PO to ixllive@ixl.com, along with the full name and e-mail address for each attendee, and we can get you registered.
What do I need to bring with me?
Every attendee must bring a laptop to the event. The entire workshop is hands-on, so it's important that every attendee has his or her own computer. In addition, we want to make sure that you'll be comfortable using IXL on the laptop you're most familiar with! Wireless internet will be provided.
I don't own a laptop. Can I participate?
Please contact us at ixllive@ixl.com to discuss ways to ensure you can participate!
Something came up, and I can no longer attend. Can I transfer my registration?
Yes, we can transfer your registration to a colleague. Please contact us at ixllive@ixl.com as soon as possible, and no later than 48 hours before the event begins. In your e-mail, please indicate your full name, the event date and location you are registered for, and your colleague's full name, school, position, and e-mail address.
I have other questions. Who can I talk to?
Please contact us at ixllive@ixl.com, and we'll be happy to help!
Do I need to register in advance for the extra hour of planning time?
No, there is no need to register in advance; everyone is welcome to return after lunch to participate!
