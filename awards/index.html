IXL awards

Knowledge isn't the only reward on IXL...

IXL is not just about helping your kids master fractions or subject-verb agreement, it's also about helping them enjoy learning. That means encouraging them to have fun! Unlike traditional workbooks and exercises, IXL offers hours of intrigue for students—without distracting them from grasping key concepts.

Uncover hidden treasures

One of the ways IXL makes learning fun is through hundreds of virtual awards that reflect kids favorite animals, foods, places, and hobbies. On IXL math, kids embark on one big treasure hunt to uncover hidden prizes, while on IXL language arts, kids try to complete their collections of stickers, stamps, balloon animals, and more!

Discover colorful worlds

Kids can wander through a winter wonderland in fourth grade, enjoy the great outdoors in sixth grade, or discover the mysteries of outer space when practicing Geometry. New challenges are gradually unlocked as kids practice, so there's something to work toward all year round. And they'll always know just how close they are to winning their next prize—the perfect nudge to keep learning!

The more you learn, the more you earn

IXL's inspiring awards and secret treasures make learning an exciting quest for knowledge. As students compete with their friends and work to uncover new prizes, they're sure to gain new skills and confidence along the way.

Join IXL today and watch your students light up with enthusiasm for learning!
