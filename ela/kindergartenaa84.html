Here is a list of language arts skills students learn in kindergarten! These skills are organized into categories, and you can move your mouse over any skill name to preview the skill. To start practicing, just click on any link. IXL will track your score, and the questions will automatically increase in difficulty as you improve!

Reading foundations

Letter identification

  1. A.1 Find letters in the alphabet
  2. A.2 Choose the letter that you hear: lowercase
  3. A.3 Choose the letter that you hear: uppercase
  4. A.4 Identify frequently confused letters

Related Games

  • Alphabet Bubble
  • Monster Mansion - Alphabet Matching
  • Alphabet Slider Puzzle
  • Connect the Dots ABC
  • Alphabetical Order Bubble

Lowercase and uppercase letters

  1. B.1 Choose the lowercase letter that matches
  2. B.2 Find the lowercase letters
  3. B.3 Choose the uppercase letter that matches
  4. B.4 Find the uppercase letters
  5. B.5 Put the letters in ABC order

Related Games

  • ABC Slider Puzzle
  • Alphabetical Order
  • Monster Mansion - Letter Match
  • Letter & Number Tracing
  • Uppercase & Lowercase Letter Matching

Word recognition

  1. C.1 Choose the two words that are the same
  2. C.2 Choose the sentence that is spaced correctly
  3. C.3 Find a word in a sentence

Consonants and vowels

  1. D.1 Sort consonants and vowels
  2. D.2 Find the vowel in the word

Syllables

  1. E.1 How many syllables does the word have?
  2. E.2 Which word has more syllables?
  3. E.3 Sort by the number of syllables

Rhyming

  1. F.1 Choose the words that have a given ending
  2. F.2 Choose the picture that rhymes with the word
  3. F.3 Which word does not rhyme?
  4. F.4 Complete the rhyme

Blending and segmenting

  1. G.1 Blend the sounds together to make a word
  2. G.2 Identify the first, second, and last sound in a word
  3. G.3 Put the sounds in order

Consonant sounds and letters

  1. H.1 Which two words start with the same sound?
  2. H.2 Find all the words that start with a given sound
  3. H.3 Which two words end with the same sound?
  4. H.4 Choose the letter that matches the consonant sound
  5. H.5 Which letter does the word start with?
  6. H.6 Which letter does the word end with?

Consonant blends and digraphs

  1. I.1 Which consonant blend does the word start with?
  2. I.2 Complete the word with the right initial consonant blend
  3. I.3 Does the word start with a consonant blend?
  4. I.4 Which consonant blend does the word end with?
  5. I.5 Complete the word with the right final consonant blend
  6. I.6 Does the word end with a consonant blend?
  7. I.7 Choose the word that matches the picture: -ss, -ll, -ff, -zz, -ck
  8. I.8 Choose the correct digraph

Related Games

  • Fuzz Bugs Farm - Consonant Blends

  4. L.4 Choose the short i sentence that matches the picture

Short o

  1. M.1 Find the short o word

  4. M.4 Choose the short o sentence that matches the picture

Short vowels

  1. O.1 Identify the short vowel sound in a word
  2. O.2 Complete the word with the right short vowel
  3. O.3 Spell the short vowel word
  4. O.4 Complete the sentence with the correct short vowel word
  5. O.5 Read questions with short vowel words

Long vowels

  1. P.1 Choose the word that has a different vowel sound
  2. P.2 Find the long a word
  3. P.3 Find the long e word
  4. P.4 Find the long i word
  5. P.5 Find the long o word
  6. P.6 Find the long u word
  7. P.7 Sort short and long vowel words

Sight words

  1. Q.1 Choose the two sight words that are the same
  2. Q.2 Choose the sight word that you hear
  3. Q.3 Complete the sentence with the correct sight word
  4. Q.4 Spell the sight word

Text features

  1. R.1 Identify book parts and features

Reality vs. fiction

  1. S.1 Which could happen in real life?

Main idea

  1. T.1 What is the picture about?

Related Games

  • The Annual Fiddlers Picnic
  • Marvin & Sweetie - Storybook
  • Marvin Makes Music - Storybook

Inference and analysis

  1. U.1 Which feeling matches the picture?
  2. U.2 What will happen next?
  3. U.3 What am I?

Vocabulary

Color and number words

  1. V.1 Use number words: one to ten
  2. V.2 Use color words

Adjectives and verbs

  1. W.1 Compare pictures using adjectives
  2. W.2 Find the picture that matches the action verb

Location words

  1. X.1 Inside and outside, above and below, next to and beside
  2. X.2 Choose the best location word to match the picture

Question words

  1. Y.1 Who, what, when, where, or why?

Synonyms and antonyms

  2. Z.2 Match synonyms

Categories

  1. AA.1 Sort objects into categories
  2. AA.2 Which one is not like the others?

Multiple meaning words

  1. BB.1 Multiple-meaning words with pictures

  2. DD.2 Capitalize the pronoun "I"

Verbs
