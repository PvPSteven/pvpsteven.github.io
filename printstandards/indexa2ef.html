  • PK.MA40 Begin to recognize, name, describe, build, and draw two and three dimensional shapes

    • Name the shape (P-K.1)
    • Circles (P-K.2)
    • Squares (P-K.3)
    • Triangles (P-K.4)
    • Rectangles (P-K.5)
    • Circles, squares, and triangles (P-K.6)

    • Cubes (P-L.4)
    • Cones (P-L.5)
    • Cylinders (P-L.6)
    • Select solid shapes (P-L.7)
    • Count sides and corners (K-V.6)
    • Compare sides and corners (K-V.7)

    • Classify and sort by color (P-G.5)
