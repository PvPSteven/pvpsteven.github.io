  • SS.2.E.1 Beginning Economics

      • What is economics? (3-H.1)

    • SS.2.E.1.2 Recognize that people supply goods and services based on consumer demands.

      • Goods and services (2-F.2)
      • Producers and consumers (2-F.3)

      • Understand quantity supplied and quantity demanded (3-I.1)
