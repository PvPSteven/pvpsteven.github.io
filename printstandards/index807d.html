Skills available for Maryland second-grade language arts standards

Standards are in bold, followed by a list of the IXL language arts skills that are aligned to that standards. Students can practice these skills online at www.ixl.com.

Standards: Common Core State Standards: Reading: Foundational skills

RF.2 Foundational Skills

  • Print Concepts

  • Phonological Awareness

  • Phonics and Word Recognition

    • RF.2.3 Know and apply grade-level phonics and word analysis skills in decoding words.

      • RF.2.3.a Distinguish long and short vowels when reading regularly spelled one-syllable words.

        • Sort short and long vowel words (2-E.1)
        • Match the short a and long a words to pictures (2-E.2)
        • Match the short e and long e words to pictures (2-E.3)
        • Choose the short i or long i word that matches the picture (2-E.4)
        • Choose the short o or long o word that matches the picture (2-E.5)
        • Choose the short u or long u word that matches the picture (2-E.6)
        • Use spelling patterns to sort long and short vowel words (2-E.7)

      • RF.2.3.b Know spelling-sound correspondences for additional common vowel teams.

        • Choose the picture that matches the vowel team word (2-G.1)
        • Complete the vowel team words (2-G.2)
        • Complete the word with the correct vowel team (2-G.3)
        • Choose the vowel team sentence that matches the picture (2-G.4)
        • Choose the words with a given long vowel (2-H.1)
        • Choose the diphthong word that matches the picture (2-J.1)
        • Complete the word with the correct diphthong: oi, oy, ou, ow (2-J.2)
        • Choose the diphthong sentence that matches the picture (2-J.3)
        • Complete words with variant vowels (2-K.1)

      • RF.2.3.c Decode regularly spelled two-syllable words with long vowels.

        • Complete the two-syllable words (2-M.2)
        • Complete the sentence with a two-syllable word (2-M.3)
        • Complete the consonant-l-e words (2-N.1)

      • RF.2.3.d Decode words with common prefixes and suffixes.

        • Identify base words, prefixes, and suffixes (2-EE.2)
        • Determine the meaning of a word with pre-, re-, or mis- (2-EE.3)
        • Determine the meaning of a word with -ful or -less (2-EE.5)
        • Prefixes and suffixes: review (2-EE.6)

      • RF.2.3.e Identify words with inconsistent but common spelling-sound correspondences.

        • Complete the words with silent letters (2-C.11)
        • Use spelling patterns to sort long and short vowel words (2-E.7)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct -ild, -ind, -old, -olt, or -ost word (2-E.10)
        • Complete the word with the correct r-controlled vowel: er, ir, ur (2-I.3)
        • Choose the diphthong word that matches the picture (2-J.1)
        • Complete words with variant vowels (2-K.1)
        • Which word has a different vowel sound? (2-K.2)
        • Sort soft and hard g words and soft and hard c words (2-L.1)
        • Pick the soft g or soft c word that matches the picture (2-L.2)
        • Homophones with pictures (2-II.1)
        • Identify homophones (2-II.2)

      • RF.2.3.f Recognize and read grade-appropriate irregularly spelled words.

        • Choose the sight word that you hear (2-O.1)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct sight word (2-O.2)

  • Fluency

    • RF.2.4 Read with sufficient accuracy and fluency to support comprehension.

      • RF.2.4.a Read on-level text with purpose and understanding.

        • Order events in a story (2-P.1)
        • Choose the picture that matches the setting or character (2-R.1)
        • Determine the topic and purpose of informational passages (2-S.2)
        • Read and understand informational passages (2-V.1)
        • Analyze stories (2-V.2)
        • Determine the themes of myths, fables, and folktales (2-V.3)
        • Use text features (2-W.1)
        • Use context to identify the meaning of a word (2-MM.1)

      • RF.2.4.b Read on-level text orally with accuracy, appropriate rate, and expression on successive readings.

      • RF.2.4.c Use context to confirm or self-correct word recognition and understanding, rereading as necessary.

        • Complete the sentence with a three-letter consonant blend word (2-C.10)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct -ild, -ind, -old, -olt, or -ost word (2-E.10)
        • Choose the vowel team sentence that matches the picture (2-G.4)
        • Choose the r-control sentence that matches the picture (2-I.4)
        • Choose the diphthong sentence that matches the picture (2-J.3)
        • Choose the soft g or soft c sentence that matches the picture (2-L.3)
        • Complete the sentence with a two-syllable word (2-M.3)
        • Complete the sentence with the correct sight word (2-O.2)
        • Use context to identify the meaning of a word (2-MM.1)
        • Use action verbs (2-RR.1)
        • Use sense words (2-VV.1)
