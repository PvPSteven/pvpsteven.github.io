Skills available for Massachusetts seventh-grade social studies standards

Standards are in bold, followed by a list of the IXL social studies skills that are aligned to that standards. Students can practice these skills online at www.ixl.com.

Standards: Massachusetts Concepts and Skills (2003)

History and Geography

  • 1 Compare information shown on modern and historical maps of the same region.

  • 2 Use correctly the words or abbreviations for identifying time periods or dates in historical narratives (decade, age, era, century, millennium, AD/CE, BC/BCE, c., and circa). Identify in BC/BCE dates the higher number as indicating the older year (that is, 3000 BC/BCE is earlier than 2000 BC/BCE)
